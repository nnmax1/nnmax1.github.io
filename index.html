Goat or Cat?

Front SideBack Side

Cat(s):0Goat(s):0

There are 3 cards in a hat. The first card has an goat on both sides. The second card has a cat on one side and a goat on the other. The third card has a cat on both sides. You draw one card from the hat and only see one side of the selected card. Then, you have to guess what is on the other side of the card.
